Terms of use

The purpose of the tool is to get familiar with Portfolio and Project Management.
The tool is supported by volentary efforts and users can not expect to get help other than from emails.
We will support you to the extend of our abbilities.
The company account is private and secure for your company and your users, we would however not recomend that highly secret or sensitive information is placed in PrioCloud.
We aim at having an constant uptime, but the system can have planned downtime, announced on this page.
For security reasons Priocloud can not create new users for an company account or help with passwords, that is for the Administrator in your company.
